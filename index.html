ABOUT

JAI SATHGURU FINE ARTS is a new sabha founded by Shri A. V. Manikantaan in Porur, aimed at promoting music and culture. With a vision to create a platform for artists worldwide, the sabha provides a stage for deserving performers without any obligations.

Shri A. V. Manikantaan, an established mridangam artist, is passionate about teaching rhythm to young students. In 2005, he started Jai Sathguru Sangeetha Vidyalaya in Adambakkam with just 10 students. Today, the school has expanded to hundreds of students and several branches across South India, supported by students, well-wishers, and music enthusiasts.

A V MANIKANTAAN was born in Chennai into a musical family. His maternal grandfather, Sri S. Gopalakrishnan, was a mridangam artist, while his mother, G. Subbulakshmi, is a music teacher and violinist. His brother, Chennai A. Srikanth Bhagavathar, is also renowned in the field of Namasankeerthanam.

Manikantaan began his musical education under the late Shri Vaideeswaran Koil Vaidhyanatha Iyer from 1988 to 1996. After 1997, he continued his training under the esteemed Shri Guru Kaaraikkudi Mani. He earned an M.A. in Rhythmology from the University of Madras in 2008 and is an "A" graded artist from All India Radio. He also received the Cultural Talent Search Scholarship from CCRT, New Delhi.
